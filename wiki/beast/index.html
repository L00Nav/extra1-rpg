The Beast

An inhuman outcast

Under the protection of Lady Luna, the star gods and the kingdom, there live the humans. Outside the borders of civilization, in the dark corners of the wilds, there lurk the cursed creatures. A simple distinction, more clear-cut than reality. Some beasts prefer looking and living like humans. Some aren't as fortunate.
