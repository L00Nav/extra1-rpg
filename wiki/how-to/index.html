The world

Clinging to its moon and the stars

Although the Sun still shines, the divine protection it once provided is long gone. Humanity is slowly losing ground as the guidance of the crown and the meager protection of distant gods is failing to keep the beasts of darkness at bay.

In the midst of such difficulties, down comes a shooting star. A shard of divinity capable of granting wishes, it is believed.
